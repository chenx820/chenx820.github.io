Compilation for Neutral Atom Quantum Computers

This project was conducted within the Quantum Operating System Group at the Beijing Academy of Quantum Information Sciences (BAQIS), where I worked as a remote research intern. In collaboration with Dr. Jingbo Wang and Dr. Xi Zhao, this work focused on optimizing compilation strategies for neutral atom quantum computing systems.

  • C. Huang, X. Zhao, H. Xu, W. Zhuang, M.-J. Hu, D. E. Liu, and J. Wang, "ZAP: Zoned Architecture and Parallelizable Compiler for Field Programmable Atom Array," arXiv preprint arXiv:2411.14037, 2024.
